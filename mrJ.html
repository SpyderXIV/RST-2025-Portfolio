Mr. J

Welcome to the page dedicated to Mr. J

As you may know by the title, this page is dedicated entirely to the man, the myth, the legend, Mr. J!

Now you may ask yourself, "Why would this guy dedicate an entire page of his RST to his computer science teacher?" Well, fair enough, I don't think many people would've done this, but theres a pretty good readon as to why I dedicated this page to him. And thats because he is easily one of my favourite teachers, and probably one of the best teachers of all time. I've known Mr.J for I believe 3 years now, starting in grade 10, having him in grade 11, and finally having him in grade 12. Now I feel like I've had ample time to really reflect on this time that I've had with Mr. J as my teacher, and I can safely say, that I enjoyed my time with him as my teacher, and I could not have wished for any other computer science teacher.

Thank you Mr. J, for everything you've done for me, and everything you've done to make my time in school, just a bit more enjoyable. :)
